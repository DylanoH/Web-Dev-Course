Who am I?



Hello! My name is Dylano Hartman and I'm from the Netherlands. I'm a Game Developer who decided to learn Web Development. I graduated from a 4 years Game Development course at SintLucas in Eindhoven. Learning is my passion and will never stop doing so. I love to code, wether it's games or web sites / applications, So I decided to learn web development as I see a better future in Web than in Games.

I also love to play games, watch anime and traveling!
