Me

My passion for art and design started at a young age which I got
from drawing with my grandfather.

As soon as I was able, I went to a school where I could learn to
become better at art and design.
This school was SintLucas.

While studying I had to find an internship and wanted to challenge
myself and at the same time gain more experience and see different cultures. Because of this I decided to go to Bangkok, Thailand for my internship, where I worked at a game company for 5 months.

Ambition project

The ambition project was the last school project we did.

the assignment was to think of something that you love which for me was strategy games.

So I decided to make concept art for 2 factions with both a starting base, a watch tower and basic housing.
